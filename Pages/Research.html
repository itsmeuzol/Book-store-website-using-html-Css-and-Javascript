INSPIRATIONS:
REFERENCE PAGE: ORIGINAL PAGE:

The inspiration for keeping best collections books in the home page along with the picture was taken from website HeritageBooks.com .The design is implied in the website of KitabSansar with little to no changes with the only minor modification for the sake of aesthetics.

The idea for the arrangement of books in Product Page was taken from Books Mandala.com The concept of keeping books on the basis of different genre was imitated from booksmandala. However, there are some change in KitabiSansar website. The color of the genre name is different. There is no hover effect on books name and description in booksmandala unlike in KitabSansar where books and description of the books pops up when hovered with mouse cursor.

The design motive for blog page of KitabiSansar was taken from the website WestBend.com .The concept of including positive as well as negative impact of technology in our lives was also inspired from this website. Similarly, the concept to include images alongside the content of the blog was also inspired from this website.

The design inspiration of the fill up form of KitabiSansar was taken from Thuprai.com .The total design of this fill up form was scripted from the website of Thuprai.com with a slight twist to the tail i.e. with added radio buttons for gender and the name input box split to two as first name and last name. Also, the part of contact infromation is added in kitabiSansar where location, number, email and social sites are given.

The overall appearance of the footer section of KitabSansar was heavily inspired from the website Books Mandala.com.The overall design of the website is implemented almost as is with only minor changes which is made to match the overall aesthetics of the website.
