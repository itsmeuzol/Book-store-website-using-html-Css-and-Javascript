Impact of Technologies in Our Daily Lives


Over the last few years, Technology has become more and more part of our lives. Take a moment to look around you. What do you notice? How many gadgets, computers and tech do you see? Did you include the one you're reading this on? See how we are surrounded by technical appliances. The laptop on your lap, the mobile phone next to your bed, the T.V screen infront of you, the WiFi router, even the headphone- all these are examples of how technology has transformed every part of our lives.
According to my view, technology is inevitable in our day-to-day lives. In today's dynamic world, life without technology is pointless. As being student of IT, I know the importance of technology in my daily life. From waking up in the morning to going back to bed in night, Everytime I am surrounded by technical equipments. The purpose of technology is to make life easier and solves many problems of mankind but has its effects all been positive? In this blog, we will precisely see that.

Fig: Technlogies in our daily life




Positives impact of technology:


Without a doubt, technology has made all our lives easier and comfortable from the past two decades. Technology has impacted our daily lives positively in different ways. Some of them are:


Smartphone revolution:

The advancement in Smartphone has simplified our lives drastically. Nowdays, people can connect with their loved ones easily and frequently through video calls and instant meeting services. Different tasks like reading and sending emails, creating documents and slides don't require a PC anymore. Additionally, the rapid expansion of e-commerce and internet-based companies is directly proportional to the rapid proliferation of smartphones.

Advanced Education:

Over the past few years, the most dramatically improved area because of technology is Education. Before the advancement of technology, we'd have to go to class physically and have to go to the library to do research. But due to Online courses, accessibility of web conference, unlimited online resources for research its been easy to work. Likewise, during pandemic when educational sectors were shut, technology played a huge role in filing the gap. Online classes made it possible for students to enroll without leaving home.

Online shopping:

Upgradement and advancement in technology have made it easier for all of us to access the internet, resulting in making online shopping popular now than ever. Online shopping makes shopping effortless and enjoyable for everyone. Nowdays people prefer online shopping without leaving the house instead of wasting hours in the shopping malls.


Negatives impact of technology:


As much as we praise technology for the convenience it brought to our lives, various aspects of it have impacted our life in many ways. Some of them are:


Diminishes Social Skills and Relationships:

As we use mobile devices more and more, we're more dependent to be virtually connected with family and friends, rather than spending time with loved oned physically. We're more likely to text, call on social media, or connect with each other via online gaming than actually meeting them in person. I am using more time on mobile battleground rather than playground. Social communication Skills are also in jeopardy due to tech overuse.

Less-active lifestyle:

While the revolution of smartphone has made our lives comfortable, it has also made us lazy. Today, entire world is at our finger, and thus we don't have to struggle to get things done like we did before. Our sophisticated lifestyle and absence of physical exercise can be partly attributed to the over-use and dependence on mobile phones. In this new era, mobile phone addiction is creating a negative impact on our lives.

Sleep issues:

We are so addicted to using tech and gadgets that we don't even leave using the devices in our bedroom. This causes intrusion with sleep in a various ways. As per the recent research and study, it has found that excessive use of technical equipments before going to bed impacts the sleep on physiological and psychological levels. Over exposure of "blue light" coming out of the screen decreases production of melatonin in body which makes it difficult for people to fall asleep. Lack of sleep makes people lethargic and less alert.


In nutshell, technology is an eminent and inevitable part our daily life. Though it have a few negative effects, it also provide a wide range of positive impacts on our daily life. Technology is both boon and bane to our life. Every aspects have positive and negative effects. The majority of the adverse effects of technology emerges from its missues. The way we handle the utilization of tech can help or harm ourselves. Instead of blaming or cursing the technology, we need to use that in a more disciplined and healthy way. Knowing about the consequences help in taking steps to recognize and reduce them so that we can make the most out of its positive impacts.



- Ujjwal Dahal, 2nd April 2022
